Macrophage polarisation in obese adipose tissue drives systemic insulin resistance via paracrine IL-1β signalling

Carobbio S, Pellegrinelli V, Vidal-Puig A  ·  Cell Metabolism  ·  2024

This study demonstrates that adipose-resident macrophage polarisation towards an M1-like phenotype during diet-induced obesity is sufficient to drive systemic insulin resistance through paracrine IL-1β signalling, independent of adipocyte dysfunction.

UCP1-independent thermogenesis in white adipocytes: role of futile calcium and creatine cycles

Bertholet AM, Kazak L, Chouchani ET, Bogaczyska MG, Paranjpe I, Vidal-Puig A et al.  ·  Nature  ·  2023

We identify and characterise futile cycling of calcium and creatine as significant contributors to thermogenesis in beige adipocytes, challenging the UCP1-centric view of adaptive thermogenesis and revealing new drug targets.

Single-nucleus RNA sequencing of human visceral adipose tissue reveals depot-specific transcriptional heterogeneity

Emont MP, Jacobs C, Essene AL, Pant D, Wely D, Vidal-Puig A et al.  ·  Nature Metabolism  ·  2022

Using single-nucleus RNA sequencing of paired omental and subcutaneous adipose biopsies, we map the cellular composition and transcriptional programmes of human visceral fat, identifying novel cell populations linked to metabolic disease risk.
