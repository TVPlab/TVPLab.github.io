Professor Antonio (Toni) Vidal-Puig

Professor of Molecular Nutrition & Metabolism  ·  Deputy Director, MRC IMS  ·  Fellow, Academy of Medical Sciences

Antonio Vidal-Puig is Professor of Molecular Nutrition and Metabolism at the Institute of Metabolic Science (IMS), University of Cambridge, and Director of the Cardiometabolic Programme at the Heart & Lung Research Institute (HLRI). His laboratory investigates the molecular basis of obesity and its metabolic complications, with a particular focus on adipose tissue biology, lipotoxicity, and thermogenesis. He trained in medicine in Valencia, Spain, and completed postdoctoral research at Harvard Medical School before joining Cambridge. He is a Fellow of the Academy of Medical Sciences, holds an ERC PI Award, and has received the Society of Endocrinology Medal and Hippocrates International Prize for Nutrition Research.
